Best Beach Resort

Best Beach Resort & Spa offers accommodations in Diani Beach. The resort overlooks the Indian Ocean and offers:

  • a 3-tier pool
  • a spa center
  • a selection of restaurants

The spacious rooms include a flat-screen TV, seating area and views of the sea or pool. Rooms are fitted with a private bathroom

For relaxation, guests can enjoy massage and beauty treatments at the spa. There is a steam room, hydro bath, hot tub and a fitness center.

Best resturants has a wide range of international and African cuisine as well as seafood dishes accompanied by a wide selection of wines.
